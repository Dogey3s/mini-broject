In our management we provide three types of decorations and arrangement options

  • Traditional stage setup
  • Thematic or Decorative stage
  • In-the-round or circular stage

Options:

Traditional stage setup

The traditional stage setup is characterized by a proscenium arch framing the stage. There is a clear separation between the stage and the audience, with the performers typically facing forward. The stage often has a backstage area hidden from the audience's view3 [Cost:300].

Thematic or Decorative stage

A thematic or decorative stage setup focuses on creating a visually appealing and context-specific environment on the stage. The design and decorations are chosen to enhance the theme or mood of the event, incorporating elements like props, backdrops, and lighting to set the desired atmosphere [Cost:600].

In-the-Round or Circular Stage:

An in-the-round or circular stage setup places the performance area at the center of the audience, with seating surrounding it on all sides. This creates a more intimate and immersive experience for the audience, as they have a 360-degree view of the performance [Cost:750].
